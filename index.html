Games

Here at 905 games we have worked hastily to not only produce an award winning Windows based game, but have branched out and have pivoted to a mobile game company. We have put all are efforts, resources and time into designing our latest release currently in beta: Goetz Run. A top down mobile adventure game where you play as the Goetz Gator and must dodge everyday object in the school halls in order to survive and beat the game.

Design

     In every product we produce, it goes through numerous changes and revise to make sure every visual aspect of it is perfect. We use the advanced hardware for visual creation and software to match. There is not a final product we produce that we aren't proud of and haven't meticulously combed through for imperfections.

Co-op

 

For this Summer we are offering a Co-op placement for interested students. the placement will be a accurate simulation of being a freelance mobile game developer, where the students work from home and report to the assigned project manager and together as a team will build an app and marketing material in a professional way, while acquiring knowledge on the freelance industry and whats its like to work with real world deadlines. The co-op placement can accompany kids of any computer science level, from advanced to novice.

About Us

      905 Games had humble beginnings starting in late august of 2017, when our founder had the idea of a mobile game based around his school. he thought and drew out his idea in late September. Work started on the companies first game "Ninja Shifters", it was a Windows PC based game and took him months to design and co-develop. the game was entered into and displayed by him at  a game making competition at the University of Mississauga, where he took 3rd best in the school board. After the high of creating Ninja shifters wore off he started to work on the mobile game he had dreamed of back in the summer. He decided to go solo on the development, design and publishing of the game, he wanted it to be a sort of right of passage. The mobile game now titled "Goetz Run" is in beta and pending publication on the Google Playstore for all Android based devices to play on. He has talked and shown the game to many in school officials and may become the official game of the school.
